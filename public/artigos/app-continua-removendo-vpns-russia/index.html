Apple continua removendo apps de VPN na Rússia

24 September, 2024

"The scale of VPN removal in Russia is worse than previously reported as VPN apps keep disappearing from the local Apple App Store." "[...]98 Russia VPN apps unavailable in the App Store" "Apple's silent removal of close to 60 VPN apps from the Russia App Store is not just alarming – it's a direct threat to digital freedom and privacy," said Benjamin Ismail, Director of the App Censorship Project.

É importante por quê: VPNs é uma das ferramentas mais importantes para combater censura, proteção de dissidentes e privacidade dos cidadãos.
