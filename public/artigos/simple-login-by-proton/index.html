Simple Login - Burner emails powered by Proton

6 November, 2022

Receive and send e-mails anonymously. With e-mail aliases, you can be anonymous online and protect your inbox against spams and phishing. Open source. Made and hosted in Europe.

SimpleLogin is open source, can be self-hosted and is 100% funded by the community. We do not use your data, track you or show you ads. SimpleLogin depends on your support to keep the service running and develop new features.
